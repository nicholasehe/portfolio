Smith Design

My internship experience at Smith Design

Overview

In the summer of 2018, I interned as a creative designer at the graphic design and branding agency Smith Design. During my experience, I mainly worked on creating Instagram posts for one of the clients, Viva La Mamma (a to-go pasta brand). While working there, I learned to ideate with a target audience, product strategy, and brand identity in mind. I learned Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects to create my designs. At the end of my experience, my designs were used by the client for its Instagram account.

Role: Creative Designer, Design Consultant

Duration: July 2018 - Aug 2018

Tools used: Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects

Instagram Post Designs

Key takeaways

  • I learned how target audiences and brand identity play a major role in commercial design
  • I learned industry-standard design tools like Photoshop and Illustrator
  • I gained experience working in a corporate environment and collaborating with team members
