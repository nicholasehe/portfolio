Overview

In the summer of 2018, I interned as a creative designer at the graphic design and branding agency Smith Design in Morristown, NJ. During my experience, I mainly worked on creating Instagram posts for one of the clients, Viva La Mamma (a to-go pasta brand). While working there, I learned to ideate with a target audience, product strategy, and brand identity in mind. I learned Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects to create my designs. At the end of my experience, my designs were used by the client for its Instagram account.

  • I sharpened my skills in industry-standard design tools like Photoshop and Illustrator
