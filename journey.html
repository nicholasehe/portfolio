Quickstart Page

The Quickstart page acts as a home page, providing the user with various options to start cycling, look at their cycling stats, or browse saved or suggested trails. On the WatchOS version, user can select workout options when starting their exercise.

Cycling Stats

Users can see their cycling stats on the Trainer page, such as their daily biking distance, average speed, and more. At the same time, they can access less in-depth stats directly on their Apple Watch.

Saved Trails and Location-Specific Stats

From the "My Trails" page, users can access various trails they've saved and their cycling history for that trail, including elevation, average speed, and more. On the WatchOS app, users can pick one of their saved trails for their workout.
